Morality Play

2014

Semester long project under the Morality Play course taught by Andrew Norman, which involves designing and implementing a interactive experience that intends to inform people on their privacy and security, particularly in the digital realm. You play through the eyes of a young man who wants to save his brother from being wrongfully arrested for a crime. Lead programmer on one of the main gameplay sections.

Cerberus

2013

Worked with an artist to create a Kinect-controlled game made in Processing. Used a skeleton detection library to allow players to puppeteer movement of a three-headed dog using the Kinect.
